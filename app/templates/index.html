ABOUT



CT-Explorer is a version of MarketNewsViz. Both MarketNewsViz and CT-Explorer are owned by Flor Lozano-Byrne and protected by Copyright laws. CT-Explorer was created to support exploratory data analysis of the Blossom Clinical Trials dataset. All Blossom Research Capital researchers are authorized to use CT-Explorer. CT-Explorer uses Blossom Clinical Trials and News datasets, gathered by Aleem Hussain Amir and Mihn Truong.
